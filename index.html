How to make the City Pretty [Div 6]

  • Leverage New Haven's Community Management Teams for neighborhood improvement.
  • Engage in or support existing beautification projects like Downtown Crossing New Haven.
  • Participate in or organize community gardening events.
  • Create neighborhood beautification programs.
  • Adhere to local zoning and other regulations when planning beautification projects.
  • Report nuisances or issues that contribute to unclean streets.
  • Partner with local governmental bodies, non-profits, and other community organizations.
  • Launch educational campaigns on street cleanliness.
  • Organize regular community cleanup drives.
  • Explore grant opportunities and other funding sources for beautification projects.
How To Make a Lemonade Stand Profitable

Lemonade Stand Checklist

  • Get Lemons
  • Research and understand local regulations regarding selling lemonade or other beverages in New Haven.
  • Contact the local health department or state agriculture office for necessary permits and licenses
  • Apply online or via mail for all required food and beverage licenses through the Connecticut State Department of Consumer Protection
  • Consider contacting a legal advisor if you have any questions regarding the permits and licenses needed.
  • Choose a location for your lemonade stand ensuring it adheres to local zoning laws.
  • Ensure your lemonade stand complies with health and safety standards.
  • Keep a copy of all permits and licenses displayed or easily accessible at your lemonade stand.
  • Adhere to any and all guidelines provided by local and state authorities.
